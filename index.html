About

Canoe sprint athlete from Serbia

I started paddling when I was twelve years old. At fifteen years of age, I have made my debut for Serbia's national team. Since then, I have won many international medals at Olympic Hopes and European and World Championships for Juniors and U23. While competing in the senior category, I have qualified to finals at World Championships, European Championships, and World Cups. I have been honored to be a part of the Serbian Olympic team at the Olympic Games in Tokyo 2020. Outside of paddling, I love programming.
